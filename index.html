OverView

Fitness trackers are equipped with a variety of sensors and technologies designed to monitor and record a range of data points. Key features typically include


  • Activity Tracking:

    This includes counting steps, measuring distance traveled, and calculating calories burned. Advanced models also track specific exercises, such as running, cycling, and swimming.

  • Heart Rate Monitoring:

    Many fitness trackers use optical sensors to measure heart rate continuously or at intervals, providing insights into cardiovascular health and workout intensity.
